Real Madrid Graduate School Universidad Europea

TRAIN YOUR DREAMS

The Real Madrid Graduate School - Universidad Europea, was founded in 2006 with the aim of training future professional leaders with hopes of working in the sports industry.

The partnership between Real Madrid, the most successful sports club of all time, and the Universidad Europea, Spain’s leading institution in comprehensive education, is intended to foster the professionalization and modernization of the sector from university education.

The Graduate School is the only educational institution that focuses its training methodology on the management areas of Real Madrid.

Real Madrid has won 23 European Cups, 13 for football and 10 for basketball; and is regarded by FIFA as the best Football Club of the 20th century. A leading club in sports, economics and society.

In recent years, it has topped the world’s most prestigious rankings, such as Forbes and Deloitte. It is also the sports entity with the widest reach on social media, exceeding 300 million users. Real Madrid is also a benchmark in charitable work. Its foundation is present in 77 countries where 95,000 children take part in its socio-educational projects.

The innovative vocation and recognized standing of the Universidad Europea spanning more than 20 years and with over 16,000 students a year, leading the Spanish private higher education market, teams up with the most renowned sports club to offer students postgraduate programs specializing in the different professional disciplines within the sports industry.

PROGRAMS THAT BRING YOU CLOSER TO YOUR PROFESSIONAL DREAMS

The postgraduate programs at the Real Madrid Graduate School - Universidad Europea, are especially designed for professionals who wish to develop their career and succeed in this sector, and who are interested in broadening their knowledge and acquiring greater experience, regardless of their original area of expertise.

The school has designed 16 specialized programs with a focus on the different disciplines within the sports industry. They can be studied in Spanish or English, in person or online. The School’s range of courses, available on-campus and online, are centered around three main areas of learning:

MANAGEMENT AND LAW

  • International Master’s Degee in Sports Law
  • Executive MBA in Sports Management
  • Specialized program in e-Sports Management

MARKETING AND COMMUNICATION

  • Master’s Degree in Communication and Sports Journalism
  • Master’s Degree in Sports Marketing

SPORTS AND HEALTH

  • Master’s Degree in Physical Exercise and Health
  • Master’s Degree in Physical Therapy for Sports
  • Master’s Degree in Football training and Management
  • Master’s Degree in Sports Training & Nutrition
  • Master’s Degree in Psychology and Sports Coaching
  • Specialized program in Sports Nutrition
  • Specialized Program in Sports Coaching and athlete’s psychology

THE WHITE WEEK

One of the main activities of the Real Madrid Graduate School - Universidad Europea, is THE WHITE WEEK. It is held in the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium and is an exclusive immersion of students at the School who throughout the week have the opportunity to live the Real Madrid experience. Students delve into and learn about the Club’s management model with top executives and members of Real Madrid and broaden their knowledge in sports management with the participation of renowned professionals with top global standing.

In addition, our collaboration agreements with more than 100 companies in the sports sector, national and international, including Adidas, Coca-Cola, Sanitas, etc., as well as football clubs and organizations such as FIFA, UEFA, CSD (National Sports Council), etc., allow students to complete first-rate internships.

THREE FUNDAMENTAL PILLARS

#1INTERNATIONAL VOCATION

The history of Real Madrid is an example of universal vocation and its pioneering spirit has inspired the creation of international competitions. Its legend in the world of football has forged the most prestigious and respected sports brand in every continent. Real Madrid is a multicultural club open to all and a sports industry leader in a fully globalized context.

Meanwhile, the university’s international vocation is demonstrated daily on its campuses, welcoming more than 25% of the students from outside Spain.

Specifically, the school annually welcomes students of more than 50 nationalities from all continents who are interested in specializing in the disciplines within sports organizations and companies directly associated with the sports industry.

Likewise, the international ambition of the Real Madrid Graduate School - Universidad Europea, has consolidated its presence in 10 countries across America, Europe, Asia and Oceania, with over 1,500 students enrolled each year and with the intention of continuing to expand internationally.

#2 EXPERIENCE-BASED LEARNING MODEL

A crucial part of the school’s academic model is based on offering students the possibility of putting into practice the knowledge and skills acquired throughout their studies. Real Madrid’s daily management translates directly into the classroom and is the cornerstone of our practical learning method. Throughout the program, students take part in real-life projects and initiatives, case studies and topical monographic workshops.

This experience-based methodology is clearly reflected in the use of first-class facilities and laboratories throughout the course:

  • Santiago Bernabéu Stadium One of the great temples of world football. It is the home ground of the world’s best football club and the place where the success model of Real Madrid has prospered.
  • Real Madrid Sports City, Valdebebas An international model of innovation, modernization and maximum sporting performance. Training center for young talent and place of work for Real Madrid’s first football and basketball teams.

#3CLOSE LINKS WITH THE GLOBAL SPORTS INDUSTRY

The connection with the sports industry becomes a reality for the students from their first day at the school. All programs are coordinated by senior executives of Real Madrid C.F., and by specialized academic directors who provide current insight into the industry.

The faculty and teaching collaborators for the school’s programs also include more than 130 professionals from Real Madrid and leading companies, federations and sports organizations in Spain and abroad, such as Adidas, Audi, Grupo Mahou-San Miguel, the Royal Spanish Football Federation (RFEF), the Spanish Sports Council, the International Paralympic Committee, Dubai Sports City or the World Football Summit. Thanks to this continuous professional environment, students are given the chance to tackle real-life cases, learn from leading companies in the sports industry, visit sporting events all over the world and receive training from university professors and prestigious multidisciplinary professionals.

ABOUT US

WE REALLY LIKE WHAT WE DO

We are an education-management company specialized in promoting the economic and academic development of universities in the USA and Spain through the creation of program partnerships and co-branded projects.


Our expertise lies in helping our partners offer unique and exclusive strategic alliances and academic programs collaborating with valuable global brands enhancing their market position and increasing their sales volume.

MISSION, VISION and Values

MISSION

In an increasingly global environment, specialization is an essential factor for success. Focusing all our efforts on what we really know how to do is what makes us different and clearly demonstrates our value proposition: we are experts in promoting the economic growth of our university partners creating Dual Degree programs to help our institutions increase their enrollment data and expand their business models.

VISION

We are very aware that, as a university, our academic programs are the image of our company in the world and, therefore, seeking excellence and uniqueness in materials can put our company on the map or make us lose any potential international recognition. Linking Education’s vision and objective is to find the perfect partner for our universities to create unique and exclusive Dual Degree Programs to promote a global benchmark in the economic and academic development of universities.

VALUES

As a university, our teaching materials, curricula or our teaching team is the first opportunity to make a good impression among our potential students and TOP companies in the labor market and, by extension, are a sample of how our company carries out its activity and is positioned in the sector. A Dual Degree program with high quality material, content and having an updated curriculum promotes, without a doubt, the overall reputation of the university itself.

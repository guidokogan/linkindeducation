WHAT ARE THEY?

Brand alliances that provide differential value to the market and our partners student network through synergies between universities in two countries with a unique and exclusive academic offer.

WHY CREATING A Program Partnerships?

Some universities may be resistant to partnering with another university that competes with them for students or profits, others may not feel the need to collaborate in new or unexpected ways. The reality of today's university landscape, however, means that partnerships are key to better serving students by merging talent, expertise, technology and purpose. Developing value-aligned partnerships that focus on common goals and complementary strengths is key to ensuring successful outcomes for all.

PARTNERING WITH LINKING EDUCATION

Benefits for universities in Spain

  • Recruitment
    Use the partner’s recruitment channel to access a greater volume of students in the United States and LATAM.
  • Business expansion
    Enter a competitive and unfamiliar market via a partner who knows, understands and dominates it.
  • Profitability
    Obtain economic profitability that the university wouldn’t attain alone with the same degree of efficiency.
  • Added-value portfolio
    Create brand alliances with American universities that provide differential value to the market and students.
  • Global positioning
    Broaden the university’s academic offer with new Program Partnerships in partnership with prestigious universities of the most attractive market.

Benefits for universities in the USA

  • Differentiation & positioning
    Establish exclusive and unique alliances in your State with world-renowned brands through remarkable Dual Degree programs.
  • Internationalization
    Enhance the university’s international cooperation and capacity building, globalize the curriculum and strengthen the prestige and profile of the institution.
  • Profitability
    Obtain economic profitability that the university wouldn’t attain alone with the same degree of efficiency.
  • Added-value portfolio
    Include a revolutionary program in your current portfolio to provide differential value to the market and your current and potential students.
  • Recruitment
    Expand business in current and different markets accessing a greater volume of students interested and attracted by our world valuable brands.
  • Revenue increase
    Partner with the private sector and experienced corporate universities to offer differentiated programs and bring in new revenue.

Benefits for students

  • Enhanced networking opportunities
    Access to network from each respective university (professors, professionals and real-world organizations), along with the connections made with classmates, is valuable for advancing a professional career. Dual Degree students experience the opportunity of creating a unique network in two different industries and this proves highly advantageous when it comes time to dive into their professional careers.
  • Broader career possibilities
    Graduating with a Dual Degree offers students a competitive advantage when searching for jobs. Our Program Partnerships can greatly improve short- and long-term career flexibility in a constantly changing job market. With two specializations, students have a broader perspective of different industries, giving them a unique professional profile and helping them gain versatility.
  • Utimate added-value education
    As technology connects companies and the job market becomes more competitive, real-world application of learned skills becomes significantly more important. The best Dual Degree programs take advantage of the businesses and corporations in their own backyard to create consulting projects, case competitions, client fellowships and management simulations — experience-based learning programs that can teach students to thrive in a competitive corporate landscape and apply skills from both of their programs of study in a dynamic environment. Combining two universities’ teachings with real-world experience provides students the ultimate added-value education.
  • Two well-positioned universities
    Students can take advantage of each school’s strengths (maybe one has a great internship program, while the other has well-equipped research facilities) to jumpstart their careers. Our Program Partnerships are hybrid programs that more accurately reflect the skills and challenges that graduates will face in the working world. Universities tap into each other’s resources, networks and offerings to better tailor the programs to students’ needs, thus developing alumni who are more satisfied, engaged, prepared and successful.
  • A more holistic profile
    Our Dual Degree students will find themselves positioned on the threshold of two different but converging areas. Students develop multiple ways of critically approaching complex issues from diverse perspectives in their programs. These holistic degrees provide our students with unique problem-solving capabilities, allowing them to apply their versatile training to diverse contexts.

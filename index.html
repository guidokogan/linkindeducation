We are an education-management agency specialized in the creation of revenue generation partnerships between universities in the United States and international corporations.

EXPERTS IN DUAL DEGREES

We develop unique Dual Degree programs where university students find a combination of the knowledge of highly ranked universities in the United States and the expertise of our partner international corporations that provide them with the tools to meet the current market needs.

ACCELERATING GROWTH

Our Partner Corporations

Universities are more and more aware of the fact that partnering with companies means adding value to their curriculum offerings. For our partner corporations, collaborating with universities is an interesting strategy to diversify their service bringing in new revenue.

That is why, we have formed alliances with some of the most valuable companies in the world to create successful academic, economic and brand positioning projects.
